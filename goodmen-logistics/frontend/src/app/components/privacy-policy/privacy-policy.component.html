1. Introduction

Goodmen Logistics LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, website, or applications, including driver onboarding and fleet management tools.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, mailing address.
  • Employment and driver information: CDL details, employment history, and data you provide in driver onboarding (e.g., employment application, MVR authorization).
  • Account and usage data: login credentials, usage of our systems, and logs necessary for security and compliance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate our services, including driver onboarding and compliance (DQF, MVR, etc.).
  • Communicate with you by email and phone (SMS/voice), including onboarding links, safety and compliance updates, and operational notices, where you have consented or where permitted by law.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations (e.g., FMCSA, safety and record-keeping).
  • Improve our services and protect against fraud or abuse.

4. Email and Phone Communications – Your Choices

You can opt in or opt out of certain communications:

  • Email: You may opt out of non-essential email at any time via the Communication Preferences page or by following the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • SMS/Phone: You may opt out of non-essential SMS and phone communications at any time via the Communication Preferences page or by replying STOP to our text messages.

Operational and legally required communications (e.g., safety-critical notices, regulatory compliance) may still be sent as necessary even if you opt out of marketing or optional communications.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with service providers (e.g., hosting, SMS/email delivery), regulators when required by law, and as needed to perform our services. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Security and Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. We retain your information as long as needed for our services and to meet legal and compliance obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information. To exercise these or to update your communication preferences, use our Communication Preferences page or contact us at the details below.
