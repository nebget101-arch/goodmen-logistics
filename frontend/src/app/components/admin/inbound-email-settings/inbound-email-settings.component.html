How to forward rate confirmations

  1. Open the broker's rate confirmation email in your mailbox.
  2. Forward it to {{ address.address }}.
  3. Keep the PDF attachment — FleetNeuron extracts load details from it.
  4. A DRAFT load will appear in your Loads list with the 📧 "Created from email" badge.
  5. Open the load to review and approve before dispatching.

Tip: Add your dispatchers' email addresses to the whitelist below so their forwarded emails are accepted.
