### FN-436 (subtask)

**Implementation Summary:**
Added AI analysis step (step 2) to the toll import wizard between Upload and Map Columns:
- After upload, automatically calls `POST /api/tolls/import/ai-normalize`
- Shows loading spinner with "AI is analyzing your CSV format..."
- Displays overall confidence banner (high/medium/low color-coded)
- Shows detected column mappings with individual confidence badges
- Pre-fills Map step dropdowns with high/medium confidence mappings (>= 50%)
- Low overall confidence (<30%) falls through to manual mapping with warning
- AI confidence badges shown inline next to each field in the Map step
- User can accept AI mapping or skip to manual mapping

**Files Changed:**
- `frontend/src/app/tolls/tolls-import/tolls-import.component.ts` — added ai_analyze step, AI state, confidence helpers
- `frontend/src/app/tolls/tolls-import/tolls-import.component.html` — AI analysis panel with confidence UI
- `frontend/src/app/tolls/tolls-import/tolls-import.component.css` — confidence badges, AI loading styles
- `frontend/src/app/tolls/tolls.service.ts` — added `aiNormalize()` method
- `frontend/src/app/tolls/tolls.model.ts` — added `TollAiColumnMapping`, `TollAiNormalizeResult`
