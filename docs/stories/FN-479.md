# FN-479: Composite Risk Scoring Engine with Automatic Triggers

## Summary
Built a composite driver risk scoring engine that calculates weighted risk scores
across seven categories with recency multipliers. Scores are persisted and
exposed via REST endpoints. Auto-trigger hooks fire recalculations after
safety incidents, HOS violations, and FMCSA scrape completions.

## Endpoints

| Method | Path | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/safety/driver-risk-scores/fleet-summary` | Fleet-wide risk summary (count by level) |
| POST | `/api/safety/driver-risk-scores/:driverId/recalculate` | Calculate composite risk score |
| GET | `/api/safety/driver-risk-scores/:driverId` | Current score + recent history |
| GET | `/api/safety/driver-risk-scores/:driverId/timeline` | Score timeline (last 12 months) |
| GET | `/api/safety/driver-risk-scores/:driverId/events` | Risk events that contributed to score |

## Scoring Formula

Weighted composite across categories:
- MVR violations: 25%
- PSP/Pre-employment screening: 15%
- DOT inspections / FMCSA violations: 20%
- Safety incidents/accidents: 15%
- Claims history: 10%
- HOS violations: 10%
- Training completion/gaps: 5%

Recency multiplier applied to each event:
- 0-6 months: 1.0x
- 6-12 months: 0.75x
- 12-18 months: 0.5x
- 18-24 months: 0.25x
- 24+ months: ignored

Score: 0-100 (Low 0-25, Medium 26-50, High 51-75, Critical 76-100)
Trend: improving/stable/worsening (compare vs 90-day-ago score, +/-5 threshold)

## Database

New table: `driver_risk_scores`
- id, tenant_id, driver_id, score, risk_level, trend, category_scores (JSONB), calculated_at, event_count

Extended table: `driver_risk_events` (added columns)
- source_id, source_table, weight_applied, recency_multiplier

## Auto-Triggers (fire-and-forget)
- safety.js: after incident created or updated (if driver_id present)
- hos.js: after HOS record created with violations
- fmcsa-scrape-queue.js: after carrier scrape completes (for matched drivers)
